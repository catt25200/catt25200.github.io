Shrine Keeper

Shrine Keeper is a top-down RPG game which a group project for my computer game development study.
It is set to a father who dies while fighting evil. And this causes those evil forces to try revenge by hurting his son.
Now player as a spirit form of the father needs to protect his son from monsters.
While doing the group project, I feel fun and challenging at the same time.
Not only because it is my first time making something quite big. I have to made bosses with different machnie and ensure different system work togehter
Also it is my first time to cooperate with people working on the same project.

Interactble Object
To encourage player to pay more attention to the surroundings and have a more interesting interaction

In this game palyer can interact with different object to gain some advantages.

In this case player can move to a easier path by breaking this tree.

There are some light as a hint for player.

Talent System

There are currently three talent tires with nine talentsin total

Player can only pick one of the three talents from each tire

Talent provides special abilities to prepare for more difficult stages later on.

Energy System

When player hits an enemy with melee, energy will be increased as the image shows.

Shooting an arrow require energy.

The energy icon will show on the top left corner.
